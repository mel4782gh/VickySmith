About

I qualified in Holistic Massage in 2004 and since then have gained additional qualifications in Aromatherapy, Sports Massage, Reflexology, Indian Head Massage, Natural Face Lift Massage and Neuro-skeletal Re-alignment Therapy.

I have also done numerous postgraduate training courses which have enabled me to work more effectively with a variety of conditions and to tailor the treatments to the individual.

I am an experienced therapist having worked at various places over the years such as K Spa in Shepherd’s Bush, Koia Health and beauty spa in Nottinghill, Neal’s Yard Remedies in Covent Garden, Nico Tailored Therapies at Fenchurch Street and the Forest Therapy Centre in South Woodford.

These experiences have further developed my ability to tailor the treatments to the individual and given me an increased confidence in the effectiveness of these treatments having seen the differences regular treatments can make to people’s lives.
